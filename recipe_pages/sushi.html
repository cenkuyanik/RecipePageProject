SUSHI

Cooking Time:

30 min.

Ingredients:

100 g Japanese rice
1 teaspoon of rice vinegar
1 teaspoon of granulated sugar
Thin slices of salmon
Steamed Shrimp
Thinly sliced sea bass and tuna
Thinly sliced cucumber and avocado

Recipe:

Wash and drain the Japanese rice with plenty of water. Take it in a special pot and cook it until it is slightly firm. Blend the rice and cool. Add rice vinegar and sugar and mix again. Prepare two-inch thick and one-inch-long meatball-shaped pieces of rice. Place sea bass, tuna, salmon or shrimp cut in the same size on the rice patties. Serve the sushi on the serving plate with pickled ginger, sliced avocado, cucumber and soy sauce. Enjoy your meal.
