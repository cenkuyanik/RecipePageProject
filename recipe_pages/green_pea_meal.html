GREEN PEA MEAL

Cooking Time:

50 min.

Ingredients:

500 g fresh peas
1 onion
1 potato
1 carrot
1 tablespoon of tomato paste
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon of black pepper
4 tablespoons of olive oil
1 clove of garlic
150 g minced meat

Recipe:

Peas are separated. The onion is chopped for cooking and put in a pot with olive oil. When the onion starts to fry a little, garlic and minced meat are added. The minced meat is well roasted. Potatoes and carrots are washed and diced. Tomato paste is added to the roasted onion and roasted until its smell comes out. Add peas, potatoes and carrots. All ingredients are roasted together. After the spices are added, water is added until it reaches the top of the ingredients and left to cook on low heat. Enjoy your meal.
