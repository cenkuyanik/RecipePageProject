LASAGNA

Cooking Time:

45 min.

Ingredients:

12 sheets of lasagna
400 g minced meat
3 tablespoons of oil
2 onions
2 tomatoes
2 tablespoons of tomato paste
2 cloves of garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1.5 cups of hot water (300 ml)
2 spoonful butter
3 glasses of milk (600 ml)
Half a glass of flour
1 teaspoon of salt
Half a teaspoon of black pepper
1 cup grated cheddar cheese

Recipe:

For the stuffing, take oil in a large pan. Let's add the onions that we cut into small pieces, after frying a little, add ground beef and continue to fry. When the minced meat is sufficiently roasted, let's add the chopped garlic and the peeled and chopped tomatoes and sauté for 5 more minutes on medium heat. Let's add the garlic and salt to it and fry it. Then, add the water and let it cook on low heat until our stuffing absorbs its water. For the bechamel sauce, put the butter and flour in a small saucepan and fry over medium heat. Let's add the milk little by little and whisk it constantly so that it doesn't get lumpy. When it starts to boil, add salt and pepper. When it reaches the consistency of custard, take it from the stove. After the minced meat sauce and béchamel sauce are prepared, let's first spread some of the stuffing in a heat-resistant baking dish. After drizzling a little béchamel sauce on it, I lined the lasagna. Let's spread it nicely by pouring a little stuffing with minced meat and then bechamel sauce on the lasagna. Let's put the lasagna sheets on it again and again let's drizzle with the inner export and béchamel sauce. Let's continue this process until you run out of materials. Let's pour the stuffing and bechamel sauce again on the top lasagna and sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese. Let's cook our lasagna in a preheated 190 degree oven for about 20-25 minutes. When it is completely browned, take it out of the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes. Then let's serve our lasagna by slicing. Enjoy your meal. Note: I recommend reading the package before making, because some lasagna may need to be boiled, if it needs to be boiled it will be indicated there
