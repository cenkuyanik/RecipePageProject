OMLETTE

Cooking Time:

5 min.

Ingredients:

2 eggs
2 slices of white cheese
3 sprigs of parsley
1 teaspoon of milk
2 teaspoons of flour
2 tablespoons of oil
1 teaspoon of salt (depending on the amount of salt in the cheese)
Half a teaspoon of black pepper
Half a teaspoon of red sweet pepper
half a teaspoon of thyme

Recipe:

In a suitable bowl, whisk the egg and milk thoroughly. Then add the shredded cheese and finely chopped parsley and continue whisking. Add the flour and whisk some more until you get a smooth consistency. Finally, add salt, black pepper, red sweet pepper and thyme and mix. Here you can adjust the salt according to the saltiness of your cheese. You can also add spices according to your taste. Then, let's take this mixture into the pan that we put oil and spread it all over with a brush, first one side and then turn it upside down and cook the other side thoroughly on low heat. A great taste for those who want to have breakfast without bread, it is very satisfying. Enjoy your meal.
