A BRIEF HISTORY OF FOODS

Humans may have started cooking their food for the first time between 1.2 million and 800,000 years ago, according to scientists who have uncovered the oldest remains of raw food.

The most distinctive remains of fire on the European continent were found in the Cueva Negra Cave in Spain, dated 800,000 years ago, and in Gesher Benot Ya'aqov in Israel. When the two settlements are taken together, the history of cooking by lighting a fire is between 800 thousand and 1.2 million years.
